## Open Source

I develop and maintain R packages for spatial and temporal analysis: [distantia](https://CRAN.R-project.org/package=distantia) (dynamic time warping), [spatialRF](https://CRAN.R-project.org/package=spatialRF) (spatial machine learning), [collinear](https://CRAN.R-project.org/package=collinear) (multicollinearity management), [memoria](https://CRAN.R-project.org/package=memoria) (ecological memory), and [virtualPollen](https://CRAN.R-project.org/package=virtualPollen) (ecological simulation).
